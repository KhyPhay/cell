If other applications can see your webcam and microphone, but Teams cannot, you may need to allow permissions for Teams. To alter permissions on Windows 10, click the Windows Search Bar (located within the left side of the taskbar) then type in Webcam. After selecting Choose Which Apps Can Access Your Camera, make sure the toggle underneath Allow Desktop Apps to Access Your Camera is switched to the On position. Then scroll past the toggle to make sure that Teams and the browser apps you use to access the web version of Teams are included in the list of apps that have been granted this permission. To alter Teams’ permissions on MacOS, click the Apple Menu Icon in the upper-left corner of the screen, then choose System Preferences. From here, select Security and Privacy, then Camera — you may need to click the lock icon to make changes here. Ensure that Teams or the web browser you are using to access Teams is enabled.
